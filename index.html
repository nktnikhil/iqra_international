Prayer Hall

Our school's prayer hall is more than just a place for religious observance; it serves as a sanctuary for reflection, meditation, and spiritual growth. In the hustle and bustle of academic life, the prayer hall provides a tranquil space where students can pause, recharge, and connect with their inner selves. Whether it's seeking solace during times of stress or expressing gratitude for blessings, the prayer hall offers students a sense of belonging and community. Beyond religious rituals, it promotes values such as empathy, compassion, and mindfulness, fostering holistic development and well-being among our students.

Computer Lab

Our school's computer lab serves as a gateway to the digital world for our students. Equipped with the latest technology and software, it provides an interactive learning environment where students can explore various applications and develop essential digital literacy skills. From coding exercises to multimedia presentations, the computer lab offers a platform for creativity and innovation. In today's technology-driven society, proficiency in computer skills is indispensable, and our computer lab plays a crucial role in preparing students for the demands of the modern world.

Science Lab

The science lab is a cornerstone of hands-on learning in our school. Here, students move beyond theoretical knowledge to engage in practical experiments that deepen their understanding of scientific concepts. Equipped with state-of-the-art equipment and resources, the lab provides a safe and controlled environment for students to explore and discover. Through experimentation and observation, students develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and analytical skills essential for success in science and beyond. The science lab fosters a spirit of inquiry and curiosity, igniting a passion for exploration and discovery among our students.
